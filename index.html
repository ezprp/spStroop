Arrows don't look at you: Qualitatively different attentional mechanisms triggered by gaze and arrows

Marotta, A., Román-Caballero, R. & Lupiáñez, J. Arrows don't look at you: Qualitatively different attentional mechanisms triggered by gaze and arrows. Psychon Bull Rev 25, 2254–2259 (2018).
DOI: 10.3758/s13423-018-1457-2

Full article available at: https://link.springer.com/article/10.3758/s13423-018-1457-2

This paper investigates the differences in attentional mechanisms triggered by social cues (gaze) versus non-social cues (arrows).
